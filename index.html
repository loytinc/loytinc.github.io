Audience Targeting is the key to connecting with the ones who will LOVE YOUR BUSINESS!

Acquiring visitors to your website by increasing traffic through online advertising has never been easier

Two of the largest global corporations (Google & Facebook) have created an array of simple easy to use tools that You Must Incorporate into your marketing

Lead Generation

The lead generation objective is designed to help you collect contact information from people interested in your business.

Utilize lead ads to collect sign-ups for newsletters, requests for follow-up calls or price quotes, or inquiries about your products or services.

In addition to requesting contact details like name or email, you can also ask custom questions to learn more about potential customers and use it to follow up with them.

^

Conversions

Crafting digital relationships that commits users to taking action

The conversions objective helps get people to take an action on your website, like viewing a page or registering for an event.

When you create a conversion ad, you'll be able to optimize your ad delivery for a specific conversion, like a lead or complete registration event.

^

Traffic

The traffic objective is designed to help send more people to your website.

Each time a person visits your website as a result of clicking your ad, the pixel fires and sends information back to Facebook.

Using the traffic objective can help you increase the number of pixel fires and collect data you can use to build audiences and optimize your campaigns.

^

Custom Conversions

Specific Categories

Custom Conversions can assist beyond standard events when a more specific breakdown is desired.

For example, a salon could separate ViewContent events into separate categories for men's services and women's services.

URL Rules

URL rules are a good choice if you do not want to update your website's code.

For instance, utilize rules to track when somebody opens a page with "/test-drive" in the URL.

Track and measure how many forms were submitted by tracking visits to a post-form thank-you page on your website.

^

Target Audience

Custom

Custom Audiences help you reconnect with people who have already shown interest in your business.

Target people who have visited or taken certain actions on your website, like viewing a page or submitting a lead form.

Lookalike

Lookalike Audiences help you find people on Facebook who are similar to your customers, contacts or website visitors.

It's a great way to expand your reach and find new people who are likely to be interested in your business.

Use a combination of Custom Audiences and Lookalike Audiences to FIND and REACH the RIGHT PEOPLE!

Find your existing customers and contacts on Facebook

Upload, copy and paste, or import your hashed customer list and match the people on your list to people on Facebook
